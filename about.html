About Our Company

Exela Cleaning Network is a fully integrated cleaning service company that provides comprehensive, high-quality, reliable cleaning solutions to commercial and residential clients in Edmonton, Leduc, Beaumont, St. Albert, and Sherwood Park.

Our diligent management and work ethic are central to our business philosophy and critical to delivering consistent cleaning services. We pride ourselves on making our management accountable to our clients through direct communication with our management team.

Our Mission

To be a national leader in the provision of cleaning services by delivering reliable, high-quality, cost-effective cleaning solutions to our clients.

Our Vision

To create a clean, safe, and enjoyable environment for our clients and build long-term relationships with them while understanding their needs and providing them with the most satisfying solutions.

Our Values

Customer-centric

Listening to and acting on our customers' feedback prevents myopic thinking and helps us constantly improve.

Integrity

We build trust through responsible actions and honest relationships.

Safety

Ensuring the safety and well-being of our employees and clients is our most important responsibility.

Teamwork

We appreciate each team member's unique strengths and encourage strong collaboration to achieve our goals.

Inclusion

We create workspace where people with differences and diverse perspectives, feel comfortable, respected and fairly-treated.

Satisfaction Guaranteed

We appreciate your feedbacks.

In the event you are unhappy with our services, we will have it amended for you.
